# Security Policy Template Structure
A formal policy document should include these sections:
* **Purpose:** Why does this policy exist?
* **Scope:** Who and what does this policy apply to?
* **Roles & Responsibilities:** Who is responsible for compliance?
* **Policy Statements:** The specific rules (e.g., password length, access rights).
* **Compliance/Enforcement:** Consequences of violating the policy.
* **Definitions:** Clarification of technical terms used.
